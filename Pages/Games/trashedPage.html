Trashed

2021, PC-Steam

Take over a disfunctional Waste Management Facility on a distant planet. Build incinerators, recyclers, robots, turrets, and more. Battle vicious creatures on the planet's surface with powerful weapons!

PLAY TRASHED ON STEAM

Trashed was released as early access on steam

Features

  • A chubby chicken
  • Explosions!
  • 2 Different and unique bosses
  • 3 unique stages of the game: Farm, City and Sky
  • More than 40 unique levels
  • A lot of fun and gold coins
