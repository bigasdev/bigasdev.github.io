Total Eggscape

2021, Android & IOs

Total Eggscape is a unique 2d game where you have to escape the farm as a Chicken!

You will face a lot of danger in your journey to the sky! Plants, creatures and dangerous spikes will try to bring you down no matter what.

About my work on this project

Roles:

Lead Developer

Work Time:

6 months

I worked as Lead Developer of Total Eggscape! Created the whole project on unity and handled every integration with the playstore + art & UI implementations. I helped with the game design as well, with the creation of the 2 bosses.

Features

  • A chubby chicken
  • Explosions!
  • 2 Different and unique bosses
  • 3 unique stages of the game: Farm, City and Sky
  • More than 40 unique levels
  • A lot of fun and gold coins
