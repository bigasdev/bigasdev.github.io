About

The foreman has had enough. He’s taken a lot of crap from the boss over at Mega Dump Corp. Now it is your turn to turn a ruined outfit into a thriving business and restore the health of the planet! As you grow your operation bigger, you will take on challenges on other planets. Beware though, a dark secret lurks on the planet’s surface.

Features:

 -Unique Waste Management system

 -Planet Terraforming

 -Day/Night System

 -Strategy based building

 -Lots of exploring, unique items to find
